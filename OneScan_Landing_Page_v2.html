The Problem

In shared spaces like universities, offices, hotels, and malls, problems happen unexpectedly. A restroom gets dirty. A light breaks. A marker doesn’t work before an important lecture. People notice the issue — but they don’t know who to contact. So nothing happens.

The Solution

OneScan is a QR-based smart communication system. Users scan a QR code placed in a space, describe the issue, and the right staff member is instantly notified. Built-in AI automatically translates messages into the staff’s preferred language.
